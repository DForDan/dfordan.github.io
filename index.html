What we do

HTS GROUP is an Engineering Solution provider specialising in delivering high quality services in our core sectors, Process, Process Safety, Electrical, Control & Instrumentation (EC&I) systems and solutions.

Why HTS Engineering Group?

HTS Automation specialise in Electrical and Instrumentation system engineering. Our competent Engineers and Designers have gained their experiences within the Petrochemical, Process and manufacturing industries. We focus on engineering and designing to the highest standards without overburdening our clients’ resource through implementing unique engagement strategies.

Selection of Capabilities

  • LV Distribution Design
  • Fully Automated Systems
  • Specification & Design of Control Panels
  • Fieldbus Engineering & Design
  • EC&I Schematics
  • Site & Infrastructure Surveys
  • Instrument Specification
  • Instrument Loop Design
  • Hazardous Areas
  • Cable Routing & Layouts
  • Commissioning
  • HAZOP Attendance
  • Design Studies and Estimating
  • IS Loop Design
  • Specifications/Scopes of Work
  • Design Schedules / Reporting
