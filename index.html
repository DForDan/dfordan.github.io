SISSuite


SISSuite is a cloud based software service for the management of SIL rated safety Instrumented functions (SIF), with the clear aim of not making life harder than it needs to be Click here.. Refinded doesn't have to mean complicated... SISSuite is "right-sized" for the job. It doesn;t overwhelm you with bloatware options you simply don't need. The emphasis is on practicability. It is also affordable - no need to play "Pass the dongle"!

Atex Inspect


Cloud based software for the management of equipment installed in potentially explosive atmospheres (ATEX). It will carry out inspections and automatically raise faults straight from your inspection. These faults can then easily have work orders assigned to them, completing the cycle. Use ATEXInspect to manage other inspections such as emergency lighting and fixed wire inspections. Click here..
