Training: Learn more about all the training HTS Group has to offer.

Advanced Functional Safety Practice - 2 Day Course

This course is for those that already have some familiarity with the functional safety standards IEC 61508/61511, but wish to expand their competence. The course is specifically designed to explore the practical concerns with implementation of the safety lifecycle. In the ‘real world’ there are a variety of constraints that make full implementation of the theoretical model impracticable; this course identifies these difficulties and shows how they may be addressed. The course is interactive and will involve a variety of exercises using software tools.

SISSuite Training - 2 Day Course

This course is specifically for those that wish to learn about the features within the SISSuite toolset and how best to make use of them. The course is interactive and will involve a variety of exercises using SISSuite. There will be incidental training on functional safety and the 61508/61511 standards, but the focus is on SISSuite as a SIS/SIF management tool. (For training specifically on functional safety and the 61508/61511 standards, please see our Introductory or Advanced Functional Safety Courses.)
