Services



Controls

HTS Controls deliver Process Control & Software solutions from consultancy through to a complete engineered system including installation and testing. Our Engineers can develop systems from various PLC and system manufacturers and are highly experienced in fieldbus solutions.

Safety

HTS Safety Solutions provide expert engineering services against the IEC 61508 / IEC 61511 standards for both new and legacy systems within the process industries. Our Engineers have a broad range of experience and are certified by TUV Rheinland to ensure the systems and consultancy services provided are in total compliance within the standards.

Site Services

HTS Site Services can deliver Electrical, Instrumentation and Mechanical installation services. Our time served installation technicians and management team can provide a complete range of packages from a standalone resource to a fully tailored solution covering both electrical and mechanical installations.



Process

HTS Process Systems deliver Functional process design and engineering for both existing and new process systems. Our Engineers can effectively deliver a project or optimise existing operations from conception through to commissioning and handover.

Automation

HTS Automation specialise in Electrical and Instrumentation system engineering. Our focus is on engineering and designing to the highest standard by implementing unique engagement strategies without overburdening our clientsâ€™ resource.
